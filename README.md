# Soul Link for Slay the Spire 2

---

## Installation

Download the .zip from the [latest release](https://github.com/doomy64/Sts2-SoulLink/releases/)

Extract the .zip

Put the extracted files into `Slay the Spire 2/mods/SoulLink`

The mod will automatically load when hosting or joining a multiplayer lobby

***All players must have the mod installed!***

---

## How It Works

### Card rewards
At each card reward, if any player takes a card, all other players have to take whichever card they have in the same position. 
Travel is prevented while any card reward stil has a forced choice

![Image showing a card reward with Soul Link enabled](https://i.imgur.com/3BPvK8w.png)

If a player takes a card from a position other players don't have (e.g. extra power from Lasting Candy), other players will be forced to skip

Extra card rewards obtained from Prayer Wheel and The Hunt can be taken freely UNLESS another player also has additional card rewards

---

### Shops
Players can't buy anything unless all players can afford their respective items in the same slot. When another player buys an item, travel is prevented until the respective item is bought.

![Image showing a shop with Soul Link enabled](https://i.imgur.com/GOnAPme.png)

---

### Events
All events are treated as collaborative events, where players vote on a single option. This affects Ancients as well, and applies even if each player's outcome is different.
